About Us / हाम्रो बारेमा

Adarsha Secondary School (आदर्श माध्यमिक विद्यालय) is located at Biratnagar-7, Morang. We offer education up to secondary and +2 levels with Science, Management, and Education programs, along with vocational and diploma courses.

Our Vision / हाम्रो दृष्टि

Nurturing future-ready students who are ethical, knowledgeable, and skilled.

Our Mission / हाम्रो उद्देश्य

Provide a safe, stimulating environment with quality instruction and extensive co-curricular opportunities.
