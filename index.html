About Us / हाम्रो बारेमा

Adarsha Secondary School (आदर्श माध्यमिक विद्यालय) is located at Biratnagar-7, Morang. Offering education up to secondary and +2 levels with Science, Management, and Education programs, the school also provides vocational and diploma courses. / आदर्श माध्यमिक विद्यालय विराटनगर-७, मोरङमा अवस्थित छ।
